About Joshua

Hi! I'm Joshua Andersson, a Swedish high school student with a strong passion for programming. I first started programming at the age of 12, learning Python online.

Ever since, I've continued challenging myself with many different languages and technologies. I also love mathematics and problem solving.

The languages I'm comfortable with are:
Python, C#, C++, C, Assembly, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SQL, PHP, Java and Ruby.
I've also used the OpenGL graphics api to write shaders and render 3D scenes.

My other hobbies

Other than programming, I enjoy playing video games. Some of my favourites are Minecraft and Factorio, where I can challenge my problem solving abilities in a rewarding environment.

Despite a lot of my hobbies being in front of a computer, I also make sure to go to the gym every other day. Part of my motivation lies in all the scientifically proven benefits, but another important aspect for me is being able to rest my brain and only focus on a single task.

I also love the fact that I live next to a forest, which allows me to frequently take long walks, where I can think about problems and relax.
